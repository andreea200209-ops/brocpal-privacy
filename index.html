🥦 BrocPal – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

BrocPal ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: email address and password (via Firebase Authentication)
  • Health & fitness data: calories, macros, water intake, sleep, workouts, fasting sessions — entered manually by you
  • HealthKit data: steps and active calories, only if you grant permission
  • Profile data: name, age, weight, height, dietary goals

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized nutrition and fitness tracking
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase (Google Cloud)
  • To generate AI-powered meal analysis via Anthropic Claude API
  • To process subscription payments via RevenueCat and Apple

3. HealthKit

BrocPal may read steps and active calories from Apple HealthKit to enhance activity tracking. We do not share HealthKit data with third parties. HealthKit data is never used for advertising or sold.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): authentication and cloud data storage
  • Anthropic: AI meal analysis from food photos/descriptions
  • RevenueCat: subscription management
  • Apple: in-app purchases and Sign in with Apple

5. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen.

6. Children's Privacy

BrocPal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
