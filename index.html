BrocPal is a personal nutrition and fitness tracking app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, how it's stored, and your rights.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your health data for advertising.

📷 Camera Access

BrocPal uses your device camera exclusively to capture photos of meals for AI-powered nutrition analysis.

  • Photos are sent securely to Anthropic's API to estimate calories and macronutrients
  • Images are not stored on our servers — only the resulting nutritional data is saved to your account
  • The camera is never used in the background
  • You can revoke camera access at any time in iOS Settings → BrocPal → Camera

🍏 HealthKit Data

BrocPal may request access to Apple HealthKit to read your step count, active calories burned, walking/running distance, and sleep analysis data.

  • HealthKit data is used only to display your activity and sleep within the app
  • HealthKit data is never shared with third parties
  • HealthKit data is never used for advertising or marketing
  • We do not write any data back to Apple Health
  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → BrocPal

🤖 AI Meal Analysis

When you use the AI food scanner, your food description or photo is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. This data is used solely to estimate nutritional information and is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

We do not store the images you submit for AI analysis. Only the resulting nutritional estimates are saved to your account.

☁️ Data Storage

Your data is securely stored in Google Firebase / Firestore, hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

  • Your data is synced across all your devices
  • Data is stored as long as your account is active
  • You can delete your account and all data from the Profile screen in-app

🔔 Notifications

BrocPal may send local notifications to remind you to log meals, drink water, or maintain your streak. These are generated on your device — no data is sent to a server. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings → BrocPal → Notifications.

👶 Children's Privacy

BrocPal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.
